OUR MISSION

Our mission is to eliminate busywork

We build software that helps teams automate manual data entry and transfer, freeing up creative bandwidth for actual, high-value problem-solving.

How it all started

FlowSync was founded in 2024 by two operations engineers who were tired of writing fragile, custom integration API scripts for startup databases. They saw that companies waste thousands of hours copying data between siloed apps like Stripe, Salesforce, Jira, and Slack.

What began as a simple command-line database sync utility quickly evolved into a robust visual automation engine. Today, FlowSync helps over 10,000 teams connect their workflows securely, with zero coding required.
